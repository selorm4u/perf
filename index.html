Family Statement

Statement From The Family

With deep sadness, the Dah and Gefia Families of Kpando Dzoanti in the Volta Region of the Republic of Ghana share the passing of our beloved Queen, Mama Yaadzee Lokkosi III. She departed on October 8th at the Korle Bu Teaching hospital, Accra, Ghana, after a brief illness. The Burial Service will be held at the Forecourt of the St. Raphael Catholic Church on August 12, 2023, at 10 am. May her soul rest in perfect peace.
